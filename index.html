What is Festi-Fab?


The Festi-FAB, as its name suggests, is a long weekend dedicated to a technological festival: fair, workshops and conferences in the field of the Internet of Things, which is none other than the automation of industrial activities. , agricultural or other of the daily life of day laborers.

  • Introduce new technologies to the public
  • Exploit the FAB-LAB of ESi
  • propose technical solutions for automation problems in the various economic fields.
  • Enable participants to improve their knowledge network
  • Attract the attention of start-ups and companies to encourage them to invest in IOT

1 : how the iOT will improve our lives and economy ?

A panel that will focus on the definition of IOT, its use in daily life in the name of: smart houses (smart houses) and how will serve people and put
them under comfort, and its use in the industrial field (controls manufacturing machines, printing damaged parts, replacing humans
with machines in certain activities, etc.) and agriculture (for example,designing an intelligent watering system).

2 : introduction to 3D printing

A panel that will focus on 3d printing: its historical aspect, the steps to print a good part that will satisfy
the customer and that will be functional, the problems that prevent this technology from developing in Algeria,
and its usefulness especially in the 'industry.

3 : internet of things and computer security

This conference will talk about one of the areas of use of the IOT: computer security. How it started, how hackers exploit these microprocessors
to create viruses and control your machine remotely, what is the relationship between IOT and networks,
all these questions will be answered in this conference.
